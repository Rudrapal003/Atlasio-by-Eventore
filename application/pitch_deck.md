# Eventore: The Future of Premium Event Planning
*A Two-Sided Marketplace for Luxury Planners and Vendors*

---

## 1. The Vision
Eventore is an exclusive, invite-only marketplace designed to bridge the gap between high-net-worth event planners and elite, premium vendors (venues, photographers, caterers). 

We are replacing the fragmented, email-heavy, and chaotic event planning industry with a centralized, luxury software experience. From discovery to escrow payouts, Eventore handles it all.

---

## 2. Market Opportunity
The luxury event planning market is booming, yet severely underserved by modern software.

- **Total Addressable Market (TAM):** $500 Billion globally.
- **Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM):** $85 Billion in North America.
- **The Problem:** High-end planners spend 40% of their time chasing invoices, negotiating contracts, and sourcing vetted vendors.
- **Our Solution:** A curated marketplace that guarantees quality and secures transactions via Stripe Escrow.

## 5. Business Model
Eventore generates revenue without charging listing fees.
- **8% Platform Commission:** Charged on every successful booking.
- **Escrow Interest:** Funds are held in escrow until 48 hours post-event.
- **Future Upsells:** Premium SaaS tools for planners (e.g., $29/mo for advanced CRM features).

---

## 6. App Store Readiness
Eventore is built on a highly scalable, modern tech stack:
- **Frontend:** React Native & Vite PWA
- **Backend:** Supabase (PostgreSQL, Realtime, Storage)
- **Payments:** Stripe Connect

The application is currently feature-complete and entering the Beta testing phase with top-tier vendors in Vancouver and New York.
